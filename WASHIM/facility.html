Healthcare Facilities

  • Washim District Hospital

    Located in the heart of Washim city, this hospital provides a wide range of medical services, including emergency care, surgery, and maternity services.

  • Primary Health Centers (PHCs)

    Several PHCs are spread across the district, offering basic healthcare services and preventive care to rural and urban populations.

  • Community Health Centers (CHCs)

    CHCs offer more comprehensive health services and serve as referral centers for patients from PHCs.

Educational Facilities

  • Government Schools

    Washim district has numerous government schools providing free education to children from primary to higher secondary levels.

  • Colleges and Universities

    The district hosts various colleges offering undergraduate and postgraduate courses in diverse fields.

  • Vocational Training Centers

    These centers provide skill development and vocational training to enhance employment opportunities.

Recreational Facilities

  • Parks and Gardens

    Washim district boasts several well-maintained parks and gardens that offer recreational opportunities and a relaxing environment for families.

  • Cultural Centers

    These centers host various cultural events and activities, promoting local art and traditions.

  • Sports Complexes

    Sports complexes are available for various indoor and outdoor sports, supporting both amateur and competitive athletes.
